# Agri-Energy Web Application
Agri-Energy is a comprehensive C# web application designed to bridge the gap between sustainable farming practices and green energy solutions. This platform serves as a digital ecosystem where farmers, energy experts, and sustainability advocates can collaborate, learn, and access resources for a greener agricultural future.

## Key Features
### 1. Sustainable Farming Hub
- **Resource Centre**: Curated collection of best practices for organic farming, water conservation, and soil health maintenance
- **Interactive Forums**: Community-driven discussion boards for farmers to share experiences and seek advice
- **Collaboration Tools**: Real-time collaboration spaces for sustainable farming initiatives

### 2. Green Energy Marketplace
- **Product Directory**: Comprehensive listings of solar irrigation systems, wind turbines, and biogas solutions
- **Comparison Tools**: Side-by-side product comparisons with technical specifications
- **Vendor Connections**: Direct linking with green technology providers
- **Review System**: User-generated reviews and ratings for energy solutions

### 3. Educational Resources
- **Online Courses**: Self-paced learning modules on renewable energy integration
- **Live Webinars**: Scheduled sessions with industry experts
- **Workshop Calendar**: Interactive training events and demonstrations
- **Knowledge Base**: Downloadable guides, case studies, and research papers

### 4. Project Collaboration & Funding
- **Project Proposals**: Platform for proposing collaborative green initiatives
- **Funding Database**: Comprehensive listings of grants, subsidies, and funding opportunities
- **Expert Matching**: Connect farmers with energy consultants and technical experts
- **Partnership Portal**: Facilitate collaborations between agricultural stakeholders

- Multi-language support consideration

### Performance
